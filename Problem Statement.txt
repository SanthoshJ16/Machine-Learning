Problem Statement: Simple Linear Prediction
A retail company wants to understand how its advertising spending influences product sales. The goal is to build a Simple Linear Regression Model that predicts Sales based on AdvertisingSpend.
Objective
Given the dataset containing 30 observations of:

AdvertisingSpend (in SEK) – Amount spent on advertising for a day.
Sales (in SEK) – Revenue generated on that day.

Your task is to:

Train a simple linear regression model to learn the relationship between AdvertisingSpend and Sales.
Use the model to predict Sales for any given AdvertisingSpend input.
Visualize the regression line (optional).

Expected Outcome
A model that fits a line of the form:
Sales=b0+b1×AdvertisingSpendSales = b_0 + b_1 \times AdvertisingSpendSales=b0​+b1​×AdvertisingSpend
